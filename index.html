Resource Calculator

This is a simple resource calculator for Cursed Tank Simulator (link)

Made by MakarTs (Discord: makargrom, Telegram: @makar_ts)

How to use:

1) Select the desired resource from the drop-down list.

2) Enter the amount of the resource in the field.

3) Click on the "Add" button.

4) When you have added all the necessary resources, click on the "Calculate cost" button to get the cost.
